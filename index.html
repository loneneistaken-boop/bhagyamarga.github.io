Our Story

A passion for travel transformed into a promise of exceptional journeys

Welcome to Bhagyamarga Holidays

At Bhagyamarga Holidays, we believe that travel is more than just visiting new places—it's about creating memories that last a lifetime. Founded with a vision to make dream vacations accessible to everyone, we specialize in crafting personalized travel experiences that reflect your unique desires and aspirations.

Our name, "Bhagyamarga," translates to "Path of Fortune," embodying our commitment to guiding you along journeys filled with joy, discovery, and serendipitous moments. Whether you're seeking adventure in exotic destinations, relaxation on pristine beaches, or cultural immersion in historic cities, we're here to turn your travel dreams into reality.

With years of industry expertise, strong partnerships with leading travel providers, and an unwavering dedication to customer satisfaction, Bhagyamarga Holidays has become a trusted name in the travel industry. We handle every detail—from flight bookings to hotel accommodations, visa assistance to customized tour packages—ensuring your journey is seamless from start to finish.

Dhairata Ojha

Founder, Managing Director & Owner

With a passion for exploration and a commitment to excellence, Dhairata Ojha founded Bhagyamarga Holidays to share the transformative power of travel with the world. Her vision of creating meaningful, personalized travel experiences drives everything we do, ensuring that every journey we curate is as unique as the travelers who embark upon it.
